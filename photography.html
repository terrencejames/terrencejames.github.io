Photography Portfolio

Although most of these shots are portraits, my photography interests are mainly in composition and creative split toning. Send me a message if you'd like to collab or want a shoot.

Most of these are shot on a SIGMA 35mm f/1.4 Art lens, but a few are shot by others and finished in post-processing by me.
